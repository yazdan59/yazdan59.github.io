Purpose of the site




Although rapid growth of GEnome-scale Metabolic (GEM) models in recent years has been considered helpful in different disciplines, it has been also increased challenges for finding a well-established resource providing previous related studies in a comprehensive framework.


So, we believe this web page tries to provide information needed for reconstruction and analysis of GEM models.

 

Surely, there has been some cases which may be missed due to diversity and interdisciplinary properties of GEM modeling approaches. Hence, we will be appreciated if you could help us by giving missed information.
